Welcome to Solidariti Pendidikan

Pemuda Harapan Negara, Pendidik Tiang Negara, Kami Berhak Berusaha

Who We Are

Solidariti Pendidikan is a student-led initiative advocating for a better education system for all by bringing facts and stories to the forefront. We aim to foster a generation of Malaysian students who are aware of their rights to education and can uplift their communities as change-makers.

What We Do

Access to Education

We advocate for equal opportunities for all students, regardless of socio-economic background.

Quality of Education

We collaborate with stakeholders to improve teaching and learning standards nationwide.

Mental Health & Well-Being

We promote supportive policies and resources that safeguard student well-being.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to ensure all students in Malaysia have access to inclusive and equitable quality education. Our mission is to advocate for systemic reforms and empower students to become agents of change.

Our Objectives

  • Address educational disparities across communities.
  • Enhance teaching and learning quality through policy reforms.
  • Promote mental health support and awareness among students.
  • Empower young people to be change-makers in education.
